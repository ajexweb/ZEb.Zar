Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 18, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Free Fire Max Tournament website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • In-Game UID: Your Free Fire Max player ID for verification
  • UTR Number: 12-digit transaction reference for payment verification
  • Payment Details: UPI transaction screenshots (processed manually)
  • Timestamp: When you submitted payment request
  • Room Credentials: Custom room ID/password (shared with you only)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Verify legitimate payments and prevent fraud
  • Assign custom room credentials to approved participants
  • Track tournament participation and joined count
  • Distribute prizes to verified winners
  • Resolve disputes and verify fair play

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No email addresses, phone numbers, or personal names
  • No bank account details or card information
  • No location data or device information
  • No passwords or login credentials

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored securely on Google Firebase (industry-standard encryption)
  • Payment records are retained for 30 days after tournament completion
  • UTR numbers are deleted immediately after payment verification
  • Only authorized organizers have access to the database

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT share your information with any third parties except:

  • Garena Free Fire support (only if required for cheating investigation)
  • Legal authorities (only if required by law)

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request to see your payment records
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data after tournament completion
  • Correction: Update incorrect UID or payment details

Contact organizers via Instagram for any requests.

7. Cookies & Tracking

This website uses minimal cookies only for:

  • Session management (popup states)
  • No advertising or analytics tracking

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page with updated date.
